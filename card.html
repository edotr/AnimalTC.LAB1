Harp Seal

Harp seals are born with fur but once they adult they "evolve" and their fur sheds. Turning them into the great large harp seal that we know today.

  • Scientific Name: Pagophilus groenlandicus
  • Classification: Mammal
  • Average Lifespan: 30 years
  • Habitat: Arctic Ocean

Harp seals are one of the many seal species out there. These seals are very social. Other places where this aquatic mammal is found, is in pop culture.
